About the Shop

YBS Magic Shop is a small business located in Levelland, Texas that specializes in jewelry handmade from rough and tumbled crystals. Products are sold locally in pop-up shops or can be picked up and delivered by request throughout the Levelland and Lubbock, Texas areas.

About the Owner

Yesenia Baeza is the owner and operator of YBS Magic Shop. Born and raised in Levelland, she's been making and selling jewelry for about 2 years now. In additional to growing her business, Yesenia's goal is to become a botanist one day. If you love any of the following, you'll definitely love what Yesenia has to offer: crystals, plants, butterflies, tarot, books, and anime.
